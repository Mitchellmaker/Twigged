Questions, answered simply.

Does Twigged need an internet connection?

After information has been saved, Twigged can find it again offline. Some Apple speech and intelligence features may depend on device support.

Does Twigged store my information online?

No. Twigged does not store your saved items online. Your information stays on your device.

Can I type instead of speaking?

Yes. Twigged includes a Type Instead option for quiet moments or when speaking is not convenient.

Can I use Siri?

Yes. Twigged supports Siri App Intents on supported devices.

Does Twigged support other languages?

Version 1.0 supports English. Additional languages are planned.
