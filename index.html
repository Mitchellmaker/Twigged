FAQ

Clear answers.

Does Twigged need an internet connection?

No. Once information has been saved, Twigged can find it offline.

Does Twigged store my information online?

No. Saved information remains on your device.

Can I type instead of speaking?

Yes. Twigged includes Type Instead for quiet moments.

Can I use Siri?

Yes. Twigged supports Siri App Intents for saving and finding items.

Does Twigged support other languages?

Version 1 supports English. Other languages are planned.
