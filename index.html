FAQ

Simple answers.

Does Twigged need an internet connection?

Twigged can work offline after information has been saved. Some voice recognition features may depend on device settings and Apple system availability.

Does Twigged store my information online?

No. Saved information stays on your device. Twigged does not use accounts, advertising or online storage.

Can I type instead of speaking?

Yes. Twigged is voice-first, but you can type instead whenever that feels better.

Can I use Siri?

Yes. Twigged supports Siri App Intents so you can save and find items using Siri.

Does Twigged support other languages?

Version 1.0 supports English only. Additional languages are planned.
