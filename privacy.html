Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Twigged, developed by MitchellMaker, handles information when you use the app.

Effective date: 11 July 2026

Information we collect

MitchellMaker does not collect, sell, rent or share personal information through Twigged.

  • No account is required.
  • No advertising identifiers are collected.
  • No analytics or tracking services are used.
  • No location information is collected.
  • No contact information is collected by the app.

Your saved information

Information you save in Twigged, such as the location of an item, is stored locally on your device.

MitchellMaker cannot access, view or recover this information.

Deleting the app may also delete the information stored by the app on your device.

Voice input

Twigged can use Apple speech recognition services to turn spoken words into text.

Speech processing is handled by Apple and may take place on your device or through Apple services, depending on your device, language and system settings.

MitchellMaker does not receive or store your voice recordings.

Typed input

You can use typed input instead of voice input. Typed information is processed by the app and stored locally on your device when you choose to save it.

Third-party services

Twigged does not include third-party advertising, analytics or tracking software.

Apple system services used by Twigged are governed by Apple's own privacy policies and device settings.

Children's privacy

Twigged does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if Twigged's features or legal obligations change. Any revised policy will be published on this page with an updated effective date.

Questions?

Contact MitchellMaker if you have any questions about Twigged or this Privacy Policy.
